Here are some of my projects

  • Jobs Reborn - Howl Addon

    Java

    Jobs Reborn - Howl Addon is a addon to the popular minecraft plugin Jobs Reborn. Jobs Reborn by itself allows players to earn in-game currency by doing their Job actions. This plugin goes an extra step and adds uniquely coded "abilities" for almost all of the jobs. These abilities allow for more diverse gameplay with the plugin, and can be paired with just about any type of server.

  • Auto Kill Phantoms

    Java

    Auto Kill Phantoms is a plugin that allows the player to not be targeted by the pesky mob Phantoms. For those who don't know, they attack you when you do not go to sleep often in the game. With my plugin enabled whenever the Phantom target the player, they will die while still dropping EXP and its drops. Allowing for a "less annoying" overall experience for the player
